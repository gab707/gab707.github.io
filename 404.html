About Gab707

Gabriel Kocher, aka Gab707, is a swiss drone pilot based in Montreal, Canada and the current runner-up drone racing world champion and Canadian drone racing champion.

He is well known for his unique video shots, capturing the world at a fast pace with very creative camera movements.

Gab707 is certified as a Part 107 operator from the US Federal Aviation Agency.

2nd place in the 2017 Allianz World Championship (DRL circuit), 1st place Canadian national championship 2016 and 2017.

3-times nominee of the New York City (NYCDFF) and Los Angeles (LADFF) drone film festivals.

Ph.D. in theoretical materials science from McGill University, Montreal.

Fly Wild

Exploring the beauty of remote wilderness.

Gab707 excels at maneuvering drones in extremely wild environments. His expertise both in high proximity flying through tight spaces and long range flying makes him one of the most experienced pilots for filming nature and wildlife from an uncommon perspective.

Wildlife Shooting

Gab707 collaborates with productions to help film wild animals in remote locations.
Racing drones are more manageable than conventional aircrafts and allow to access untouched tree-filled or craggy areas.
